Yuni Sari Amalia, S.S., M.A., Ph.D

Lecturer at Universitas Airlangga | Faculty of Humanites| English Department

Yuni Sari Amalia is currently a Deputy Director for Planning and Development Board, Universitas Airlangga and Lecturer at the English Literature Department, Faculty of Humanities Universitas Airlangga, Indonesia. She began her academic world Universitas Diponegoro Indonesia taking Bachelor Degree in English. Then, she pursues her master degree at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Department of Curriculum and Instruction. Finally she received her PhD from Department of Curriculum and Instruction, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
